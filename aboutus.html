About Us

The Best Equipment - The Best Service - The Best Staff

In 2016, AlpineKo opened its doors as BCIT's #1 source for skiing/snowboarding equipments.

We believe our services such as lessons, rentals, and tune ups are truly a step above. Our goal is to deliver the most exceptional service so that you can have the best experience on the mountain as well as with us. Whether you are a beginner or an expert at skiing/snowboarding, we have the perfect equipments for you. Also, our wonderful team of instructors can provide you with lessons at a pace that's comfortable for you. Not only can we provide you with lessons and rentals, but also tune ups upon request.
